Global Wonders in 3D

CONTEXT

I utilized Adobe XD to design a seamless and intuitive UX for "Global Wonders in 3D." The responsive layout ensures easy booking and purchasing for VR adventures, prioritizing a captivating user journey.

Using Adobe XD, I crafted an immersive interface for "Global Wonders in 3D." The design evokes wonder and adventure, enhancing users' exploration of diverse cultures and stunning destinations in 3D.

Problem Statement

Many individuals, including those with disabilities and financial constraints, face barriers in experiencing the world through travel. These barriers limit their ability to embark on vacations or explore diverse cultures. Additionally, some lack the financial means to access high-quality virtual reality (VR) experiences that could provide an alternative. "Global Wonders in 3D" aims to address this gap by creating an inclusive platform, ensuring an immersive and accessible 3D exploration for everyone, regardless of physical ability or financial limitations.

User Pain Points:

  • Difficulty accessing affordable and high-quality virtual reality experiences.
  • Complex and cumbersome booking processes for VR adventures.
  • Limited options for inclusive and accessible 3D exploration, excluding people with disabilities.
  • Lack of engaging interfaces for discovering and purchasing captivating 3D journeys.

Iterating Towards Our MVP

In the iterative process towards our Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for "Global Wonders in 3D," we began by identifying the essential features that align with our vision of a captivating and accessible 3D exploration platform. We prioritized functionalities that directly addressed user pain points, focusing on streamlining the booking process for VR adventures and ensuring inclusivity for all users, regardless of financial constraints or disabilities. Through continuous design and development iterations, we refined the user interface to be engaging and intuitive, aiming for a seamless user journey from exploration to purchase. Our MVP embodies these critical components, forming a solid foundation upon which we can build and enhance the platform to provide an exceptional 3D experience for our users.
